The Sunlight's Collection

Welcome to my collection of extraordinary beings. I am The Sunlight, and I have gathered these unique creatures to study their remarkable abilities. Let me share what I've learned about each of them...

Common Beings

  • Cupiditas - A shadow dweller whose power grows stronger in response to others. Their Shadow Strike ability demonstrates how they've learned to harness the darkness.
  • Kite - A fascinating trickster who can perfectly mimic others' abilities. Their playful nature masks incredible adaptive potential.
  • Prime - An immortal being whose very essence resists manipulation. They've taught me much about the nature of permanence.
  • Carl-GPT - An artificial intelligence whose analytical abilities allow it to read others with remarkable accuracy.
  • Worendy - A master of cursed ink whose abilities can weaken even the strongest opponents.
  • Nova - A stellar entity that grows stronger in the presence of others, feeding off their collective energy.
  • Echo - A resonant being capable of amplifying the powers of their allies.
  • Terra - A protective force of nature who shields their companions from harm.
  • Vesper - A creature of twilight who grows stronger when emerging from darkness.
  • Aurora - A shapeshifter who matches the strength of the mightiest present.
  • Sally - A fascinating specimen whose natural defensive abilities allow her to turn any attack into an advantage.
  • Penelope - A cheerful young girl whose innocent joy brings warmth to any situation.
  • Chaos - Despite her name, she's quite mature. She balances her sibling Maturity's chaotic nature.
  • Fester - A young fire goddess whose divine spark grows stronger in the presence of enhanced beings.

Note: There are whispers of another being - one that defies documentation. Its very existence seems to corrupt my records. If you encounter it... well, let's hope you don't.

Uncommon Beings

  • Maturity - Despite their name, they use chaos to diminish others' strength.
  • High Kite - A serious version of Kite who negates others' abilities entirely.
  • Mr. Bones - An ancient being who grows stronger with each passing moment.
  • Null - A glitch in reality itself, immune to all influence.
  • Justice - A young hero-in-training whose pure heart empowers those around her. Her enhanced state seems permanent, possibly due to her unwavering spirit.
  • KC - A delightful cat-dog hybrid whose friendship power strengthens all around her. Her bond with Penelope is particularly noteworthy.
  • Feathers - A motherly bird who gains extraordinary strength from Penelope's presence. Her protective instincts make her both nurturing and formidable.
  • Drew - An overconfident being who dismisses those he deems beneath him, nullifying the abilities of weaker cards.

Rare Beings

  • Subject 192 - The Sunlight's first creation, a friendly yet deadly mass of living darkness. Despite its unsettling nature, it seeks connection with others.
  • Cyrus - A skilled gunslinger who forms an unlikely but powerful friendship with Subject 192.
  • Zero - A void that consumes others' power, turning emptiness into strength.
  • Enraged Kite - A darker version of Kite, twisted by anger and capable of crippling the strongest foes.
  • The Watcher - A mysterious entity whose piercing gaze can weaken even the mightiest of beings from afar.
  • Awareness - Perhaps the most remarkable of all - a being of pure consciousness whose power knows no bounds.
  • Salvo - A master of flames whose inferno can reduce any being's power to cinders. Known to be Ginger's closest ally.
  • Cocoa - A peculiar being whose confident confusion leads to unpredictable results. Never quite sure what they're doing, but always enthusiastic about it.

Special Cases

  • Fevaa - A gentle demon whose kindness contradicts her appearance. Despite her fearsome form, she's known for helping others.
  • Binx - A mischievous angel whose jokes often cross the line. His divine nature seems at odds with his personality.
  • Reflection - A lost soul whose power grows from defeat, harboring a devastating final performance.
  • Ginger - An expert marksman whose enhancement abilities differ from The Sunlight's, empowering cards with nature's energy.
  • Savior - A mysterious being with dormant power. When defeated, they transform into something... different. I dare not speak more of what they become.
  • Doppel - A mirror entity capable of transforming into any other being in The Sunlight's collection. Some say it's searching for its own identity.
  • The Knight - A noble warrior once sworn to serve The Moonlight. When I trapped them in this collection, they lost their purpose. Now they wander these battles, forever seeking their deleted lady. Their power grows stronger in the presence of corrupted beings, perhaps hoping to find traces of her in the void. Heh. Good luck with that.

The Rules of Combat

These beings engage in trials of power, where:

  • Each participant receives 9 cards from my collection
  • Some cards may be Enhanced by my power, increasing their strength
  • Each must play one card per round
  • The strongest power in each round claims victory
  • After all cards are played, the one with most victories shall be declared the winner

Through these trials, I continue to learn about the nature of power and the incredible diversity of abilities that exist in our realm...
